Technical Leadership & Delivery

Architecture connected to implementation

Lead roles across mobile, full-stack, GIS, and product work support a practical form of technical leadership: understand the system, find the highest-risk boundary, make the architecture legible, and stay close enough to implementation to validate the decision.
