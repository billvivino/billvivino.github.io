How Billing Works

We keep payments simple with a rolling retainer—no third‑party platforms or hidden fees.

  • You pre‑fund one week of projected hours before work begins each Monday.
  • We accept secure ACH credit and Zelle® transfers (full payment instructions are provided on each invoice).
  • Every Monday you receive a detailed timesheet and balance statement; simply top the retainer back up for the coming week.
  • Any unused retainer balance is 100 % refundable within five business days of project completion.
