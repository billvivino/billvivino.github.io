Why the sprint is capped

The intro sprint is meant to reduce risk at the beginning of a relationship, especially when the codebase, business constraints, or technical path are still unclear.

By capping the first engagement at 10 hours, we keep the work focused: inspect the real system, solve or isolate the highest-leverage issue, and decide what should happen next.
