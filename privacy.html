Privacy Policy & Data Protection

Last updated: April 15, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes what personal information we collect on our website, how we use it, and how you can control that information. Our aim is to be transparent and give you control over your data. By using our site or submitting information, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to give us through our contact forms. This includes:

  • Name: so we know what to call you.
  • Email address: so we can reply to you.
  • Phone number: if you want us to call or text you.
  • Country: to understand where you are contacting us from.

If you send us a message through our forms, you may also provide additional information in the message itself, which we will receive along with the above details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide only for the purposes described below:

  • To respond to your inquiries or requests and provide the information or assistance you asked for.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer support based on the inquiries and feedback we receive.

We will not use your personal information for any purposes other than the above without your consent. In particular, we will not send you marketing emails or newsletters unless you have explicitly signed up for them.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use any cookies or tracking technologies on our website to collect your personal data. The only third-party tool we use is Google Tag Manager, which helps us manage website code and monitor basic site performance. Importantly, Google Tag Manager does not collect any personal information about you. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics or advertising trackers. Because we do not track our visitors across third-party sites, our website does not respond to browser 'Do Not Track' signals. In short, when you visit our site, we are not storing cookies on your device or tracking you beyond your visit to our site.

Sharing of Your Information

We respect your privacy and we will not share your personal information with third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. The details you submit through our contact form are used only by us to assist you. We do not disclose your information to any third-party companies or individuals, except if required to do so by law (for example, if a law enforcement agency legally requires us to share certain information). Essentially, your information stays with us and is used only for its intended purpose.

Data Security

We take precautions to protect your personal information. Our website is secured with standard security measures (such as HTTPS encryption) to ensure that the details you send us are protected during transmission. We also maintain safeguards to protect the information once we receive it, and we continuously work to keep our systems updated and secure. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information.

Data Retention

We will keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose you provided it for (for example, to respond to your inquiry) and for our legitimate business or legal requirements. Once we no longer need your information, we will delete it or anonymize it so that it no longer identifies you. If you would like us to remove your information from our records sooner, you have the right to make that request (see the "Your Rights and Choices" section below), and we will honor it.

Your Rights and Choices

You are in control of the personal information you share with us. If you have submitted a contact form, you can request at any time to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you (if any).
  • Update or Correct your information if you believe it is out-of-date or inaccurate.
  • Delete the information you have given us, so it is removed from our records.

To make any of these requests, simply reach out to us using the contact information below. We will be happy to assist you and will respond as soon as possible. There is no charge for these requests.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years old. If you are under 13, please do not provide any personal information to us. In the event that we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. If we make significant changes, we will let you know by posting a prominent notice on our website or by other means. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this Privacy Policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy whenever you visit our site to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, please feel free to contact us. You can reach us by email at our contact form. We are always here to help and will respond to your inquiries as quickly as possible.

Thank you for trusting us with your information. We are committed to keeping it secure and respecting your privacy.
